Local Recycling Guide

In Austin, curbside recycling collection services are available for residential buildings with four or less units. Recycling is collected every other week in blue bins, and any surplus recycling is also permitted. Austin Recycling accepts most paper, plastic containers, hard plastics, cardboard, metals, and glass.



Questions? Visit the Austin Recycling Site, or Contact us.
